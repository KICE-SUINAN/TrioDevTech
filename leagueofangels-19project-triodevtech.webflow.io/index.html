LEAGUE OF Angels: CHAOS

“League of Angels: Chaos” is set in a world engulfed by chaos due to Ragnarök. The heavens themselves have been torn asunder. The world is at the brink of destruction following a war that has ended all hopes of peace and prosperity. The world is suffering from constant attacks, and moral chaos replaced order, awakening unspeakable horrors in all corners of the world.

You, as a champion of the gods, are the last hope to bring back order and restore the realm. With the power to summon angels down to earth, you are the only one who can put an end to this madness. Your journey is to become a legend and drag this war-ravaged world out of chaos and revive order back to this chaotic land.
